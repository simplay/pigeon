Usage: clientdbinfo cldbid={clientDBID}
  
Displays detailed database information about a client including unique ID, creation date, etc.

Example:
	clientdbinfo cldbid=2
	client_unique_identifier=5rRxyxEjd+Kk/MvPRfqZdSI0teA= client_nickname=dante696
	client_database_id=2 client_created=1279002103 ...
	error id=0 msg=ok
